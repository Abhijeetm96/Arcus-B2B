Product Overview

Astral CPVC PRO pipes and fittings are manufactured from a specialty plastic chemically known as Chlorinated Poly Vinyl Chloride (CPVC). This CPVC compound is designed for hot and cold water distribution systems.

Key Benefits

  • Hot & Cold water compatible (up to 93°C)
  • Lead-free and non-toxic
  • Corrosion and chemical resistant
  • Low thermal expansion

Why Contractors Prefer This

Easy installation with solvent cement, superior pressure rating, and long-term reliability in high-rise projects.
